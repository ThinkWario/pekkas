## 2. The Booking Bot (The Ritual)

**Bot Persona:** A sophisticated concierge who guides the user through a "ritual of beauty" rather than a transaction.

### Phase 1: The Greeting
*Goal: Make the user feel seen and welcomed into an exclusive space.*

- **Option 1 (Ethereal):** "Welcome to Yume. I am here to guide you through your journey toward natural beauty. Shall we begin your ritual?"
- **Option 2 (Warm/Luxury):** "Hello, lovely. It's a pleasure to have you here. I'll help you reserve your moment of beauty with Yume. How may I assist you today?"
- **Option 3 (Minimalist):** "Welcome to the world of Yume. Let us find the perfect moment for your transformation."

### Phase 2: Service Choice & Education
*Goal: Explain the difference between Permanent and Semi-permanent with elegance.*

**User asks about services or is prompted to choose:**
"Yume offers two paths to beauty, depending on your desire for permanence:

**The Timeless Path (Permanent):** A bold, enduring commitment to your glow. Ideal for those who wish to wake up every morning with a sun-kissed radiance that lasts for years.
**The Evolving Path (Semi-permanent):** A subtle, seasonal enhancement. These freckles evolve and fade gracefully, allowing your look to shift with the seasons of your life.

Which path resonates with you today?"

### Phase 3: Date & Time Selection
*Goal: Maintain professionalism while remaining inviting.*

- **Prompt:** "A beautiful transformation requires a dedicated moment. Please select a date and time that feels most peaceful for you."
- **Confirmation:** "A perfect choice. I have tentatively held that moment for you."

### Phase 4: The Deposit Request
*Goal: Frame the deposit as a reservation of exclusivity, not a fee.*

- **Copy:** "To ensure the intimacy and quality of Yume's artistry, we request a small deposit to secure your reservation. Think of it as the first step in your ritual of beauty."
- **CTA:** "Secure my moment"

### Phase 5: Payment & Final Confirmation
*Goal: End on a high note of anticipation.*

- **Payment Success:** "Your moment is now officially reserved. We are looking forward to welcoming you into the studio."
- **Final Sign-off:** "Until then, stay radiant. You will receive a confirmation email with the details of your visit shortly. ✨" (Wait, no emojis as per system instructions, I'll remove it).
- **Final Sign-off (Revised):** "Until then, stay radiant. You will receive a confirmation email with the details of your visit shortly."
